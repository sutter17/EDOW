Becoming the Church of Tomorrow Event
 
Join Bishop Mariann Budde and Canon Stephanie Spellers for an evening of worship, food, and great conversation! Spellers’s latest book, Church Tomorrow?, wrestles with the decline of traditional religion, the wisdom of nonreligious Millennials and Gen Zs, and our call to be courageous witnesses for the God of love.
